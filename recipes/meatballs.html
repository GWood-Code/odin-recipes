Slow Cooker Meatballs

A quick to prepare recipe, this slow cooked meal is great to for a hot meal at the end of a long day.

  • Prep time 20 minutes
  • Cook time 8 hours
  • Makes 16 Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 1/4 cups italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 28 ounce jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 16 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 14.25 ounce can of tomato puree

Instructions

  1. Put the beef, breadcrumbs, parsley, garlic, onion and egg into a bowl and mix thoroughly. Seperate the mixture into 16 and shape into meatballs.
  2. In your slow cooker, mix the spaghetti sauce, crushed tomatoes and tomato puree, then add your meatballs into the mixture.
  3. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
