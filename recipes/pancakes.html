Breakfast Pancakes

A great way to start your day, these pancakes are sure to become a family favourite.


  • Prep time 5 minutes
  • Cook time 20 minutes
  • makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tspn baking powder
  • 1/4 tspn salt, or more to your taste
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar into a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the centre and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter, and mix until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over a medium-high heat. Pour or scoop approximately 1/4 cup of batter into the frying pan. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
